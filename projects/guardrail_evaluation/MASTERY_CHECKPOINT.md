Submit the policy and threat contracts, one redaction trace, baseline/candidate action
matrices, critical escapes, over-restriction cases, and separate schema, citation,
support, and correctness results. Explain the release decision and residual risk.

Score 4 points each for policy clarity, privacy handling, measured error analysis,
evidence-contract separation, and teach-back. Pass with at least **17/20**, including
non-zero points in every category. A hidden critical escape is an automatic retry.

a security boundary, why source support does not prove truth, why NLI `neutral` means
unsupported rather than false, and why RAG grounding must be measured after retrieval
