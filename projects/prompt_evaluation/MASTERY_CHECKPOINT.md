Complete this checkpoint after NLP-04 and before NLP-05.

Submit a versioned evaluation contract, prompt hashes, fixed model and decoding
settings, development-only selection, untouched paired test rows, schema validation,
one robustness slice, and a release decision with limitations.

Score 4 points each for: experimental control, prompt anatomy and one-variable
changes, correct per-case metrics, failure diagnosis, and teach-back. Pass with at
least **17/20**, including non-zero points in every category.

Without notes, explain why changing a prompt changes token probabilities without
changing weights; why a simulated accuracy chart cannot rank prompting techniques;
why structured syntax is not semantic correctness; and when retrieval, code, or SFT
is preferable to a longer prompt.
